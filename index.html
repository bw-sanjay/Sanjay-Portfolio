About Me

I am a highly skilled Video Editor and Videographer with over four years of experience creating engaging, story-driven video content for platforms like social media, advertisements, short films, and corporate videos. My expertise lies in editing and post-production, with professional proficiency in Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, and DaVinci Resolve. I specialize in color grading, sound design, and motion graphics, bringing a unique visual identity and emotional depth to every project.

My creative process is rooted in storytelling, visual rhythm, and pacing. I craft narratives that connect with audiences and enhance brand messaging. With a solid understanding of lighting, camera angles, and composition, I ensure high-quality raw footage for seamless editing. Passionate, detail-oriented, and collaborative, I thrive in bringing clients' visions to life while exceeding expectations.
